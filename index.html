About Us

Who We Are

A Liberian-owned enterprise committed to transforming infrastructure and logistics across Liberia and beyond.

Since2020

Elite Express Logistics Liberia.

Established and legally registered in the Republic of Liberia, Elite Express Logistics Liberia. (EEL) is headquartered in Central Sanniquellie, Nimba County. We were founded to contribute to Liberia's infrastructure development, mining support services, and economic growth through reliable construction and logistics solutions.

As an emerging enterprise, we are strategically positioned to support road construction, building construction, haulage operations, logistics coordination, and mining-related support services. EEL is committed to building a reputation founded on safety, integrity, professionalism, and operational excellence.
